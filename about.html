Professional Summary

Transforming complex data challenges into innovative business solutions

I am a passionate Data Science professional with a unique blend of economic theory and advanced analytics expertise. With a Master of Arts in Economics from Bangalore University and certifications from DataMites™, IABAC, and nasscom FutureSkills, I specialize in transforming complex datasets into actionable business insights that drive measurable business growth.

Previously, as a Data Science Consultant at Rubixe, I leverage machine learning and data science to deliver tailored, client-focused solutions. I have executed successful proof‑of‑concepts, collaborated with cross‑functional teams, and improved client satisfaction by deploying scalable ML solutions.

My journey in data science includes the development of InsightfulPy, a Python library with 25+ automated EDA functions published on PyPI. My work has reduced manual effort by 40% and accelerated reporting speed by 25% through intelligent automation and robust model design.

I excel in building end-to-end data pipelines—from data collection and preprocessing to model deployment and performance monitoring. My domain expertise spans financial modeling, customer analytics, employee performance analysis, and predictive maintenance.

I worked as a Coordinator at Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail Ltd., where I was responsible for accurate financial transaction processing and resolving discrepancies in sales reports, strengthening my attention to detail and business acumen.

I am driven by a strong passion for applying data science to solve real‑world problems and deliver insights that enable strategic decision-making.
